The Opportunity

Why African Cooperatives Are the Most Undermonetized Fintech Vertical on Earth

Africa has over 63 million registered cooperative members. Nigeria alone has more than 22,000 registered cooperative societies managing billions of naira in monthly contributions. Yet almost all of this financial activity flows through manual ledgers, Excel sheets, and informal cash handling — completely invisible to banks and completely unmonetized by software vendors.

Coopify is a Nigerian SaaS platform that has digitized this workflow. It has barely scratched the surface of what is possible. This explores the opportunities that can be unlocked by layering embedded payments, lending, and insurance on top of the existing software, to transform from workflow tools into full financial operating systems — unlocking 40 to 120 percent ARR uplifts within 12 months.

💡 Key Insight: The company that builds the Cooperative Financial Operating System for Africa — owning contribution data, loan flow, payment infrastructure, and credit intelligence simultaneously — is not building a SaaS business. It is building Africa's alternative banking system from the bottom up.
